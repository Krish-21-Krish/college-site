About the College

A centre of excellence committed to transforming lives through education and innovation.

Who We Are

Dr. SNS Rajalakshmi College of Arts and Science is an autonomous institution dedicated to holistic education, research, and societal impact.

With a strong focus on innovation and design thinking, the college nurtures students to become industry-ready professionals and responsible citizens.

Fast Facts

  • Established in 1999
  • Autonomous status with strong industry tie-ups
  • Diverse UG, PG, and research programmes

Vision

To be a globally recognized institution fostering academic excellence, research, and innovation for social transformation.

Mission

To provide quality education, promote interdisciplinary learning, and empower students through value-based, technology-driven pedagogy.

Accreditations & Highlights

  • Accredited by reputed national bodies.
  • Strong placement support and industry collaborations.
  • State-of-the-art infrastructure and digital learning ecosystem.
